## What is Kobble?

Kobble provides a plug-and-play solution for developers to easily monetize and distribute APIs. Our platform acts as a Gateway between your users and your API and includes a built-in Customer Portal, many Advanced Analytics and a powerful Monetization feature using Stripe Connect.
