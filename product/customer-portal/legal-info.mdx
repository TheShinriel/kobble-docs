---
title: Legal Information
description: Add your legal information to your customer portal.
---

You can add legal information to your customer portal, such as your terms of service, privacy policy, and cookie policy.

To add legal information:

1. Open your Kobble dashboard and select Customer Portal in the left menu.
2. Select the [Legal settings tab](https://app.kobble.io/p/portal/legal).
3. Update the privacy policy, terms of service, and cookie policy.

The legal information will be displayed in the footer of your customer portal.
